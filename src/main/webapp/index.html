About IgniteOne

IgniteOne is a web-based crowdfunding and project showcase platform built for students, alumni, and recruiters. It allows students to pitch innovative ideas, receive funding support, and gain visibility among potential mentors and employers. Recruiters can discover talented individuals and support real-world innovation right from campus.
